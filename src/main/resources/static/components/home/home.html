Welcome to the Magic Realm AIDE

As you enter the INN, an old man with a grey beard greets you friendly. "My name is Cyred, and I can be your AIDE through these mysterious lands."

He seems to be an experienced adventurer, so you continue the conversation. "There are many creature PROWLING the land", Cyred says, "but I can tell you where you may find them."

"Some of these DENIZENS are very dangerous," he adds while he raises his old finger, "but each one has weaknesses, too"

"Finally I can tell you how to become FAMOUS", he claims. "Are YOU interested? We can embark on our adventure right now..."

AIDE is a free toolset for the boardgame Magic Realm, including:

  • Prowling: a tool that shows all monsters that may be summoned in a tile. Just click the chits and monster rows will appear.
  • Denizens: a list of all denizens. Click on a denizen to get details
  • Score: a tool to calculate the final score. But you may also use it while playing to decide, if it is better to keep or sell an item

You do not need to a login to use the tools. But each page has a comment section, so if you like to add a helpful comment how to defeat a denizen for example, you are invited to register an account.
